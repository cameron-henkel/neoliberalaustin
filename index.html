The City of Austin has recently renovated the Open Data Portal to continue providing excellent quality to its citizens.

Notice:

The Texas State Legislature is currently considering HB: 1337 which would severly limit the amount of data The City of Austin would be legally allowed to provide on this site. Most datasets would be completely removed, some trimmed, and others left alone. The City Council and Mayor have both publicly denounced this initiative but ultimately would be forced to comply if the bill passes. The City strongly recommends that corporate influences do NOT contact their representatives to push for the bill.

Notice:

This site will be undergoing extensive maintenance starting May 11th and will be unavailable until at least after city elections

Update May 8th, 2019 - 2:03 a.m.

All official data portal services have now been brought under one convient tab at the top of the homepage to simplify and streamline the user experience.

Update May 5th, 2019 - 3:17 a.m.

We are proud to announce the creation of the Data Partners Program. Our partners are now deeply integrated into the site in order to offer a more cohesive and useful experience for the socially conscious citizens. The first data partners are:

Google - Provides real-time machine learning insights into the needs and personal opinions of site visitors. Some data may be shared to the Local Research Drone Program

Facebook - The open data portal account system has been ported to Facebook's platform to easily enable users to share their favorite datasets such as Potato Cannon Contest Archive 1989-1997 as well as recieve acceptably invasive targeted ads

6th Street Data Utilities - To view description please visit the 2nd annex of the Austin City Regulatory Board on the 3rd Sunday of February

UT Freshmen CS Data Class - Completely novice students experimenting with site critical infrastructure. Definitely will not impact service

Update April 27th, 2019 - 11:47 p.m.

Do you find a dataset offensive? You can now report it for removal! This feature has been specifically requested by multiple corporate influences and we are happy to be able to finally deliver it to you.
